### Communication Procedures


* Our team will communicate primarily through text messaging.
* Discord will be used for meetings
* Microsoft Team/Planner will be used for planning team and individual responsibilites.
* It is expected that team members respond withing 2-3 hours of text communication. 
* Lia Ryan will email the instructor for any team correspondence that is required. This will be communicated to the rest of the team by copying their email addresses and informing them of the info shared through text.


### Meeting Schedule and Coordiation


* Team will meet every week Monday and/or Friday at 7-8 PM. 
* Additional meetings will take place on Sunday from 2-5 PM if needed.
* It with be a virtual meeting over discord, unless we need the advantages of an in-person meeting.
* Reminders for the meetings will be through Google Calender
* Changes to this schedule will be by informing the team of our desire to change the meeting time at least 24 hours in advance of said meeting. Then we create a When2meet to determine the new meeting time.
* Team members are expected to respond to text communications within 2-3 hours with a reaction or response. Exceptions will be made for nights, previously informed busy times, and emergencies. Team members are expected to be more active within 48 hours of a due date.
* Team members are expected to take an anonymous bi-weekly form to report any difficulties or conflicts with other team members, which we will review together in a team meeting.


### Team Coordination & Accountability

* Assignments are submitted after each teammate is signed off on the assignment and their contribution withing Microsoft Planner or if its within an hour of the due date.
* To Ensure that each team member has the knowledge and skill required, the team will have a bi-weekly team performance form which will be reviewed in the following meeing, team members can discuss problems or concerns about their work and we will help address them or allocate more resources to overcome the problem. Additionally, team members can voice their concerns and issues during any meeting.
* Feedback will be reviewed by each team member an implemented. A week after the feedback has been received, team members will review each others work to ensure feedback has been implemented.
* Missed contributions/assignments/actions will be addressed in our bi-weekly google form to bring up issues in a timely manner.
* We will hold one another accountable to the expectations described in this charter through our anonymous bi-weekly google form.
* Recognizing that a team member is underperforming will be done through our bi-weekly google form.
* We will help an underperforming team member improve by discussing it in our team meetings to come up with a solution.
* If a team member is not improving within a one/two week period, the rest of the team will nominate them for a pink slip.

#### Conflict Recognition & Resolution

* Any conflicts that occur during the semester within the team will be brought up in the bi-weekly form will be discussed during meetings to determine the proper action to take to fix the conflict.
* If a problem brought up in a bi-weekly review is not resolved by the next review, or a team member is not conforming to the above expectations, we will escalate the issue to the instructor and pink slips may be necessary.
